# Policies

How to work with DJ Sprenk & their music.

For any questions, clarifications, or special cases, [get in touch](/contact)!

## Music Usage Policies

_V2.1 - Last updated 2026-07_

> **What This Covers:** Playing Pre-Recorded Music from DJ Sprenk

If you want to play a set from DJ Sprenk at an event where **no money** is
involved, let the music play! You can always [support](/support) the DJ by
tagging on social media or leaving a tip.

_This includes events like house parties without a cover, practice sessions, and
free community socials._

For events with **any** sort of fee, you _should_ be [tipping](/support#tipping)
the DJ, regardless of whether the event is profitable or not.

_This includes events like paid socials and socials with recommended donations._

A recommended tip is **1-2x the entry / ticket / donation amount**.

### Special Case – Classes, Warmups & Supervised Práticas

An exception is made for warmups, supervised práticas, and class backgrounds.
These are cases where the central focus is the instruction and where a DJ would
not be hired anyways; turn it up!

### Weekenders / Larger Events

Festivals and weekenders require special licensing agreements. You must
[get in touch](/contact) before using any music from DJ Sprenk in these
contexts.

## Booking Policies

_V2.0 - Last updated 2026-06_

> **What This Covers:** Hiring DJ Sprenk to Perform Live at Your Event

### Services

Available services can be found on the [services](/services) page.

### Compensation

Fees and hours are agreed upon per event.

In addition to the DJ fee, the client agrees to provide a full event pass,
round-trip travel, lodging (with a private bed), and meals over the event dates.

### Travel

Travel is typically arranged round-trip from Boston Logan International (BOS)
and must include a carry-on and personal item.

Always confirm travel plans with the DJ before booking anything.

### Equipment

The DJ requires a table, a nearby power outlet, and an audio interface with an
open ¼", ⅛", or RCA port.

The DJ is not responsible for providing speakers, microphones, or any other AV
equipment unless arranged in advance.

### Scheduling

DJ sets may be scheduled for no shorter than 30 minutes at a time and no longer
than 2 hours at a time, with no more than 4 total working hours in any rolling
24-hour period.

### Cancellations

Cancellations are accepted up to an agreed-upon window before the event.

Cancellations closer to the event date require reimbursement of all
non-refundable expenses.

Cancellations within 24 hours of the event start or after DJ travel has begun
(whichever comes first), require full payment of the DJ fee.

### Payment

Payment is due within one week of the event via cash,
[PayPal (@djsprenk)](https://paypal.me/djsprenk), or
[Venmo (@djsprenk)](https://venmo.com/djsprenk).

Late payments are subject to a $20/week surcharge.

### Promotion

The client agrees to promote the DJ using only materials from the provided
[media kit](/media-kit), once booking is confirmed in writing.

The DJ will help promote the event in return.

### Performance Recordings

The DJ retains full rights to any recorded sets, including sole discretion over
whether and how to share them.

Copies may be shared with the organizer upon request, but may not be
redistributed or posted publicly.
