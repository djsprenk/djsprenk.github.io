# Music Policies

My guiding principle: music is meant to be shared, and I want my sets to be
accessible while ensuring the work that goes into them is respected.

## Personal / Free Use

You may use my sets for personal listening at no cost. Share links freely.

## Paid Events

For events where a door fee is charged, a licensing fee applies. The standard
rate is 1–2x the door fee, payable via Venmo or PayPal.

## Exception – Classes, Warmups & Supervised Práticas

Instructors may use my sets for classes, warmups, and supervised práticas
without a licensing fee. This exception does not extend to social events or
festivals.

## Community Events

Community-run events with low or no door fees are handled case-by-case. Reach
out and we'll work something out.

## Weekenders / Larger Events

Festivals and weekenders require a licensing agreement. Please
[contact me](/contact) before use.

## Previous Client Arrangements

If we've worked together before, your existing arrangement stands unless we've
discussed otherwise.
